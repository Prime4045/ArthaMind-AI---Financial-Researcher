# Financial Research AI Agent Development Project - Dual Track Version

**Team Size:** 3-4 students  
**Duration:** 8 Weeks  
**Choose Your Challenge Level**

---

## Project Overview

Build an AI-powered financial research assistant that analyzes stock markets, economic trends, and provides personalized investment insights through intelligent data integration. You'll master financial APIs, sentiment analysis, and risk assessment while creating a production-ready fintech application.

- **Domain Focus:** Financial Markets & Investment Analysis
- **Core Skills:** Financial data processing, sentiment analysis, risk modeling, regulatory compliance

---

## Choose Your Track

### Track A: Essential (Recommended for Most Teams)

- **Focus:** Core financial analysis skills, achievable goals, strong fundamentals
- **Domain:** Stock Analysis OR Personal Finance Helper
- **APIs:** 2-3 basic integrations (Yahoo Finance + News API + Economic data)
- **Framework:** Standard LangChain with financial tools
- **UI:** Streamlit with financial charts
- **Deployment:** Single platform (Streamlit Cloud)
- **Data:** Basic stock analysis and news sentiment

### Track B: Advanced (For Confident Teams)

- **Focus:** Industry-ready fintech skills, complex financial modeling
- **Domain:** Comprehensive Financial Research Platform
- **APIs:** 5+ integrations with fallback strategies and rate limiting
- **Framework:** LangGraph for complex financial workflows
- **UI:** Professional React/Next.js with advanced charting
- **Deployment:** Multi-service architecture with real-time data
- **Features:** Portfolio optimization, risk modeling, regulatory compliance

## Frequently Asked Questions

- **Indian Stock Symbols:** Use `.NS` (NSE) / `.BO` (BSE). Market hours: 9:15 AM – 3:30 PM IST.
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Provide analysis, not advice. Add disclaimers.
- **API Costs:** Track A uses free tiers; Track B requires caching + rate limiting.
- **Switching Tracks:** Allowed from B → A after Week 4.
- **Market Holidays:** Handle closures with cached/historical data.
- **Cryptocurrency:** Optional for advanced teams; focus on traditional markets first.
